Start with lukewarm water to rinse sweat. End with 30 seconds cool water. Reduces muscle inflammation. Signals body to sleep mode.
RECOVERY
10:30
🍳 Optional: 1 Boiled Egg If Truly Hungry
Only if stomach is actually growling. Maximum 1 boiled egg. Nothing else. No bread, no rice, no biscuits. Don't eat if not hungry.
OPTIONAL
10:35
📵 Phone Down — Wind Down
No TikTok. No YouTube. No scrolling. Lower brightness. Talk to Amni, read, just rest. Blue light kills melatonin and ruins your sleep quality.
SLEEP HYGIENE
10:45
🌡️ Sleep Environment Setup
Fan on. Room dark or dim. Comfortable clothes. 7 hours sleep = 6am wake. Poor sleep = higher cortisol = more belly fat stored. This is science.
SLEEP PREP
11:00
😴 SLEEP — Lights Off
Target asleep by 11pm. Sleep is not optional — it is half the fat loss battle. You recover, repair muscle, and burn fat while sleeping. Protect it.
